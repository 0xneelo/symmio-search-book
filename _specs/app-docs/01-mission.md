# 01 — Mission, Definition of Done, Operating Principles

## Mission
Build the **definitive compendium of intent-based derivatives** — a **unified manifesto of the intent-based derivatives revolution**, authored as a **joint effort of the Symmio and Vibe teams and the wider researcher community**. It does two things at once:
1. **Makes the case** — the vision, history, and thesis of intent-based derivatives (why they matter, why now, why Symmio started in 2021, why Vibe bet on permissionless derivatives for *any* token).
2. **Is the reference** — the complete, precise, navigable documentation of how Symmio and Vibe actually work, end to end, down to the Vibe dashboard's revenue / volume / points.

The front door is an **answer engine**: a question goes in, the exact page comes back. The docs **onboard you into the system** — they sense what you came to understand and take you deep. And they are **living**: every question and rating feeds back to make them better. Target: **500-800 substantive, cited pages**, the best documentation in web3 — rivaling Stripe/Twilio for craft and bettering anyone for depth of *why*.

**Scope:** a **unified joint compendium** (Symmio × Vibe), presented as the combined work of both teams + researchers — a single manifesto + reference, not a merge of two existing doc sites.

## Definition of Done
- A built, deployable docs site of **500-800 substantive, cited pages** spanning **the manifesto (the "why")** and **the reference (the "how")**.
- An **answer-engine front door** (`06`): ask → routed to the exact page → rate the answer → ask again.
- A **living-docs loop** (`06`): every question tracked, every answer rated, gaps surfaced, demand-driven improvement.
- The **full narrative/thesis** (`02`): vision, history, intents-vs-order-books, tokenization, bootstrapping, Hyperliquid/HIP-3, solvers, isolation, UX, long-term vision.
- The **product/reference layer**: revenue (incl. the venue/chain/phase matrix), volume, points, vibe-points, onboarding/referrals, every dashboard view.
- The **Symmio Discord mined/imported** (community questions + Lafa answer candidates) into the FAQ/answer-engine demand seed (`07`) as internal-only evidence; exact public Discord/Lafa statements require editorial review before publication.
- Every claim **traceable to a primary source**; contradictions reconciled or logged. **No secrets leaked.**
- `DECISIONS.md`, `SOURCES.md`, `STYLEGUIDE.md`, `GAPS.md`, `QUESTIONS.md` (tracked-question log), a page manifest, and a final report committed.

## Operating Principles
- **Primary sources over memory.** Read the actual code, the actual docs, the original + current whitepapers, the Discord. Quote and cite. Never invent a mechanic or a number.
- **Argue *and* document.** This is a manifesto and a reference — make the case with conviction *and* be precise. No hand-waving; every claim sourced.
- **Reconcile, don't paper over.** Investigate disagreements (e.g. the 5-vs-15 referral-level discrepancy); log unresolved ones in `GAPS.md`.
- **Cite everything.** Each page lists its sources; maintain `SOURCES.md`.
- **Safety.** Public protocol docs, whitepapers, and on-chain addresses are fair game; our private backend URLs, API keys, admin tokens, and env values are NOT. When unsure, omit and note it.
- **Demand-driven.** Let tracked questions + the Discord Q&A decide which pages to write and how to structure them. Real questions shape the IA.
- **Parallelize research, centralize voice.** Many agents research; ONE information architecture and ONE style guide, so it reads as a single, authoritative work.
