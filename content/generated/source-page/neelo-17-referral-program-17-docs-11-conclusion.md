# Section 11: Conclusion

The referral program is not a standalone marketing widget.
It is a structural layer that connects user acquisition, market creation, and fee distribution.

From the transcript, one conclusion is consistent:

**the system must optimize for listings and sustainable fee flow, while preserving trust under adversarial conditions.**

That requires:

1. clear identity and attribution boundaries,
2. hardened reward accounting,
3. explicit anti-gaming policy,
4. phased rollout discipline,
5. and transparent KPI governance.

If those constraints are respected, referral mechanics can evolve from access gating to a durable growth engine:
- creator-aligned,
- partner-compatible,
- and economically auditable at scale.
