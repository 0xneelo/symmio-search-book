# Section 6: Programmatic Graduation

Once Vibe exists as a discovery layer, listings can become rule-based.

Instead of asking whether a market feels worthy of listing, the system can ask whether it has met measurable graduation criteria:

- sustained perp volume
- trader count
- liquidity depth
- healthy liquidation behavior
- evidence of two-sided participation
- declining need for solver support

At that point the next listing slot can be triggered mechanically, whether on an internal order book, an intermediate venue, or directly on Hyperliquid.

This is the real promise of Part II: not just better listings, but **programmatic graduation**. Markets move upward because they have matured, not because someone liked their story.
