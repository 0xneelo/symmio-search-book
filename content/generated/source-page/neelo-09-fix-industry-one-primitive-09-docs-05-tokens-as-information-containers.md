# Section 5: Why Derivatives Matter

Spot markets are powerful, but they are incomplete.

A spot market lets participants buy an asset. A derivatives market lets them express a fuller view: long, short, hedge, basis trade, or simply hold a position on whether an asset deserves sustained value. That second layer is what turns raw issuance into real market structure.

This is why the missing primitive is not just tokenization itself. Tokenization already happened. What is missing is the ability for any token to immediately gain a derivatives layer.

Once derivatives exist, a token is no longer only a container for ownership or narrative. It becomes an object of full price discovery.
