## 1.7 Scope and Limitations

This paper focuses on **cryptocurrency market infrastructure** and **listing dynamics**. We do not cover:

- Regulatory considerations (jurisdiction-dependent)
- Traditional finance listing processes (different dynamics)
- Token economics or valuation (out of scope)

We assume readers have familiarity with:
- DeFi primitives (DEXs, AMMs, bonding curves)
- Perpetual futures basics
- Major crypto protocols (Binance, Uniswap, PumpFun, Hyperliquid)
