## 5.3 Practical Deployment Playbook

1. **Pilot phase**
   - start with small LP allocation from treasury/insiders;
   - monitor directional skew, funding transfer, and turnover quality.

2. **Validation phase**
   - produce weekly PnL attribution and drawdown reports;
   - verify that returns are not solely one-off directional luck.

3. **Controlled scale-up**
   - increase capital in predefined tranches;
   - use stop conditions tied to drawdown, skew instability, and liquidity quality.

4. **Steady-state operation**
   - maintain target utilization bounds;
   - keep treasury exposure limits and emergency controls documented.
