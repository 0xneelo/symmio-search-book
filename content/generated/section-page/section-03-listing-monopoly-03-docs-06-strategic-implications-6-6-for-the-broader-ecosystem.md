## 6.6 For the Broader Ecosystem

### 6.6.1 Completing the Lifecycle

Filling the gap completes the token lifecycle. Benefits:
- More efficient capital allocation
- Better price discovery (two-sided markets)
- Reduced fragmentation between early and late stages
- Pathway from fair launch to institutional

### 6.6.2 Reducing Systemic Issues

The transcript notes that the gap "creates a lot of the issues we have in the crypto market." Filling it may reduce:
- Volatility (hedging available earlier)
- Manipulation (short pressure)
- Disconnected price discovery
- Fragmented liquidity

### 6.6.3 Long-Term Vision

At scale (e.g., $10B+ market cap), tokens may reach ETF consideration. A complete lifecycle—from creation through perps to institutional—creates a coherent path. The gap was a discontinuity; filling it creates continuity.
