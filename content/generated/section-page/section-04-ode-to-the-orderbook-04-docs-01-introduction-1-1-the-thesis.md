## 1.1 The Thesis

Vibe improves order book DEXs by doing what they cannot efficiently do themselves:

1. Bootstrap perpetual markets for low-cap assets
2. Measure genuine trader demand
3. Harden token lifecycles through two-sided price discovery
4. Feed pre-qualified markets into order book venues

In short: **Vibe discovers demand; order books scale efficiency.**
