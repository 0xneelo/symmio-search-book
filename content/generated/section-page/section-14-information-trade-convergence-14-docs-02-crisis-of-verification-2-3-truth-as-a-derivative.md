## 2.3 Truth as a Derivative

This fundamentally changes how humans interact with reality. We stop being "readers" or "viewers" and start being **speculators on truth**.

| Old World (Web2) | New World (Web3 + AI) |
|------------------|------------------------|
| Information is abundant and free | Information is infinite and suspicious |
| We trust Experts and Brands | We trust Liquidity and Staked Value |
| Fact-checking by journalists | Fact-checking by short-sellers |
| Reality is consensus of opinion | Reality is consensus of capital |
