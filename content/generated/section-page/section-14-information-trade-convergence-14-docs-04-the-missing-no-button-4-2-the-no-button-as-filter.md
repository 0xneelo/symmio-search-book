## 4.2 The NO Button as Filter

**Scenario**: An AI generates a fake video of a CEO resigning. 1,000 bots buy the token; price skyrockets; the "lie" becomes "profitable reality" for a few hours.

**With shorting at launch**:
- The bots buy the fake news token. Price goes up.
- One actor (human or AI) realizes it is deepfake.
- Instead of ignoring it (Web2), they **short it with 10× leverage** (Web3).
- The price crashes. Bots lose. The "Truth Defender" profits.

**Fact-checking becomes a profitable trade**, not a charitable act.
