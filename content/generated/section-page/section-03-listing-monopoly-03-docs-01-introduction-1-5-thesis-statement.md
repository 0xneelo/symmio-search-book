## 1.5 Thesis Statement

**Control over the token lifecycle—specifically, control over listings at each stage from creation through derivatives—is the primary source of monopoly power in cryptocurrency. The protocol that enables permissionless perpetual markets for the long tail of tokens will fill the last gap in the lifecycle and capture the corresponding monopoly position, connecting fair launch ecosystems with institutional infrastructure.**

We will support this thesis through:

1. A token lifecycle framework mapping where power concentrates
2. Analysis of existing listing monopolies (Binance, PumpFun, Uniswap, Hyperliquid)
3. Identification of the critical gap between DEX graduation and perp availability
4. Strategic implications for filling that gap
5. Thielian monopoly analysis of listing control
