## Part 2: The Small Market Strategy

### Thiel's Principle

> "You want to go after small markets. If you're a startup, you want to get to monopoly. Monopoly is you have a large share of a market. How do you get to a large share? You start with a really small market and take over that whole market, then find ways to expand in concentric circles."

> "The thing that's always a big mistake is going after a giant market on day one because that's typically evidence that you haven't defined the categories correctly."

### Vibe Trading Analysis

**The Market Definition Question**:

Could Vibe make the classic mistake of saying "We're in the $100B+ perpetual futures market"?

**Correct Market Definition**:
Vibe's actual initial market is **not** all perpetual futures. It's specifically:

> **"Perpetual markets for tokens in the $60K-$50M market cap range that cannot currently get perp listings"**

This is a small, definable market that:
- Has no current solution
- Is underserved and overlooked
- Can be dominated quickly
- Has clear expansion paths

**Concentric Expansion Path**:

```
CIRCLE 1 (Launch):
Meme coins on Solana launching via PumpFun
- Market size: Small but definable
- Competition: Zero (no one serves this)
- Goal: 80%+ market share

CIRCLE 2 (Expansion):
All new tokens across major L1s
- Requires: Multi-chain deployment
- Competition: Still minimal
- Goal: Dominant position in "new token perps"

CIRCLE 3 (Growth):
Mid-cap tokens ($50M-$500M) wanting better perps
- Competes with: GMX-style protocols
- Advantage: Better efficiency as markets mature

CIRCLE 4 (Maturation):
Order book integration / Data services
- Symbiotic with: Hyperliquid, CEXs
- Revenue: Graduation fees, data licensing

CIRCLE 5 (Dominance):
The "perp creation infrastructure" layer
- Position: Essential infrastructure for all derivative markets
- Moat: Data, relationships, operational expertise
```

**Key Insight**: Vibe is NOT competing with Hyperliquid for existing perp volume. It's creating a new category: **"perp market infrastructure for market creation."**

**Thiel Test**: ✅ **PASS** — Small, defensible initial market with clear concentric expansion.
