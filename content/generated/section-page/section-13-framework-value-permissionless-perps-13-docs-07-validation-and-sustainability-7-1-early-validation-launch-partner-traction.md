## 7.1 Early Validation: Launch Partner Traction

### 7.1.1 The Metrics

Vibe has onboarded **160+ launch partners** prior to full product launch—before a live dashboard, before profit payouts were operational, and before permissionless listings were enabled. Projects have deposited **millions in token value** based on the design and proposition alone.

### 7.1.2 What This Signals

This traction indicates:
- **Structural demand** exists for the value proposition (token deposit → perp market → revenue share)
- **Projects are willing to commit** before seeing realized returns
- **The design resonates** with token holders and treasury managers
- **Alternative options are worse** (CEX listing, MM hire, Uniswap single-sided)—projects choose Vibe

### 7.1.3 Pre-Revenue Commitment

Partners committed without:
- Live trading volume
- Historical LP yield data
- Payout track record
- Permissionless listing (at time of commitment)

Commitment was based on **design**, **value alignment**, and **comparative advantage**. This is a strong signal of perceived value.
