## 8.3 Implications

### For LPs
Token holders can earn yield on held assets with low incremental risk. The 70% revenue share and risk alignment make participation attractive relative to alternatives.

### For Traders
Perp access for long-tail tokens; execution and payout certainty; lifecycle improvement as markets mature.

### For Projects
Utility for tokens without stablecoin commitment; treasury deployment; ecosystem support; revenue share.

### For the Ecosystem
Permissionless perps; capital efficiency; lifecycle completion; price discovery and hedging for long-tail assets.
