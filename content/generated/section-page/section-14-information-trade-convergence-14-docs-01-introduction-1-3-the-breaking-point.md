## 1.3 The Breaking Point

We are at a breaking point. We are losing connection to reality. With infinite synthetic information being created by anyone, the old verification mechanisms fail. Experts can be faked. Brands can be fabricated. Polls and journalism are cheap words.

**The thesis**: The only way to trust information in the future is if it is **tokenized and has a market cap attached**.

This is the theory of **costly signaling** applied to the digital age. When information becomes free and infinite, it becomes worthless—and dangerous. To restore value and trust to information, we must attach a financial cost to it. We must shift from Proof of Authority to **Proof of Value**.
