## 7.5 Risks to Sustainability

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Trading volume below expectations | Focus on high-interest tokens; launchpad partnerships |
| Solver hedge effectiveness | Operational expertise; pre-hedging; dynamic pricing |
| LP exit (token price crash) | Risk alignment (holders stay); revenue share incentive |
| Competition | Structural advantages; 160+ partners; first-mover in category |
| Regulatory | Transparent design; rule-based; no opaque LP structures |
