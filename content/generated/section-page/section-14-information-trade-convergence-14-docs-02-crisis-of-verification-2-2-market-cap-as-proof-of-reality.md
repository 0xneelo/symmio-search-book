## 2.2 Market Cap as Proof of Reality

If you cannot trust the content itself (because an AI could have made it), you must trust the **commitment** behind it.

**Skin in the Game**: If someone posts "information" and creates a token for it, they expose it to the market. If the market believes it is true, they buy; market cap rises. If it is proven false, value collapses to zero.

**The Wager**: A high market cap becomes a signal: "A large group of people have bet their own money that this is real."

**Price = Credibility.**
