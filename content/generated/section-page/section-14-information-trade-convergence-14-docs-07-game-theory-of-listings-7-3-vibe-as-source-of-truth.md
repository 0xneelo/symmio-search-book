## 7.3 Vibe as Source of Truth

Our protocol can act as a source of truth for listing agents—or even mechanically lead to listings. Example pipeline:

1. Token launches on launchpad
2. Once token reaches $60k, it moves to DEX
3. **Immediately** gets a perp on Vibe Trading
4. Once perpetual volume on Vibe reaches $50M daily, it gets listed on HIP-3 / any orderbook
