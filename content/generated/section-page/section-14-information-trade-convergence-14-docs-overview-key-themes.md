## Key Themes

### The Convergence
- **Information**: AI enables infinite synthetic information; cost of creating "truth" drops to zero
- **Trade**: PumpFun enables permissionless asset creation; attention is instant securitization
- **The Crossroads**: We are losing connection to reality; the only verification is market cap

### Market Cap as Truth
- Shift from Proof of Authority to Proof of Value
- Price = Credibility; Truth becomes an asset class
- Fact-checking done by short-sellers, not journalists

### The Missing Ingredient
- Ability to vote "NO" on tokens (short)
- Permissionless futures at launch
- Leverage allows truth-knowers to overpower hype-mobs

### The Strait Gate
- "Control the token lifecycle and you control the future of information"
- Vibe as filter between launchpad chaos and Tier 1 order
- "Because strait is the gate... and few there be that find it" (Matthew 7:14)
