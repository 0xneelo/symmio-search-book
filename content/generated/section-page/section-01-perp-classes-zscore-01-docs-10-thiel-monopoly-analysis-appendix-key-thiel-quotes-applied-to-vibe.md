## Appendix: Key Thiel Quotes Applied to Vibe

> "If you're copying these people, you're not learning from them."

Vibe isn't copying Hyperliquid or GMX—it's doing something neither can do.

> "All happy companies are different because they're doing something very unique."

Vibe's hybrid architecture is genuinely unique in the market.

> "You must begin by studying the end game."

Vibe's end game is clear: become the infrastructure layer for all derivative market creation.

> "The first mover in chess is someone who plays white... You want to be the last mover who wins the game."

Vibe may not be the first perp protocol, but it could be the last word in market creation.

> "Don't always go through the tiny little door that everyone's trying to rush through. Maybe go around the corner and go through the vast gate that no one's taking."

Everyone is trying to compete with Hyperliquid on mature markets. Vibe goes through the gate of market creation that no one else has found.
