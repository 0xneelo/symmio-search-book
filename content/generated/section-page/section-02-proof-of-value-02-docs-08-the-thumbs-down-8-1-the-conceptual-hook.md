## 8.1 The Conceptual Hook

For 20 years, the internet was built on the "Like" button. Web2 optimized for engagement: if you Like something, it grows. If you hate it, you comment (engagement)—it grows more. Web3 (Crypto 1.0): optimized for "Number Go Up." You can only buy (Long). If you hate a token, you ignore it. Ignoring does not kill it; it leaves a zombie.

**Result**: A reality distorted by toxic positivity, fake engagement, and infinite synthetic slop.
