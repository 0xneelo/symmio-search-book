## 4.4 Evolution Requires Predators

Evolution requires predators. In the current ecosystem, scammers are the predators and retail is the prey. If you add shorting, you introduce a new apex predator: **The Short-Seller**. These predators hunt for fake AI news, scams, and vaporware. They aggressively drive the price of "Fake" information to zero to make a profit. By acting selfishly, they perform a public service: cleaning the timeline of lies.
