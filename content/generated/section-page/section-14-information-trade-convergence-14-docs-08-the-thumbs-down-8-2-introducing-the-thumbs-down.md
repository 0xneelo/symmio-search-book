## 8.2 Introducing the Thumbs Down

We are introducing the **missing half of the feedback loop**: financializing the "Thumbs Down."

**The Pitch**: "Restoring the Immune System of the Internet"

**The Diagnosis**: The internet is sick. It has no immune system. When a virus (scam, fake narrative, hallucination) enters, the body has no way to kill it. We can only ignore it. Viruses multiply when ignored. We have built an economy of "Yes Men"—a machine that can only say "Long."
