## 1.1 The Historical Analogy

The British Empire pursued a simple strategic objective: **control the sea, and you control the world.** Maritime dominance meant control over global trade routes. Because all significant trade flowed through the oceans, sea control translated directly into economic and political dominance. The strategy worked—for centuries.

This paper applies the same logic to cryptocurrency: **control the token lifecycle, and you control the crypto industry.**

In crypto, value flows through listings. A token has no economic existence until it can be traded somewhere. The protocols and platforms that determine *which* tokens get listed *where* and *when* control the flow of capital, users, and attention. They are the sea lanes of the digital asset economy.
