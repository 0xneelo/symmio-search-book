## 1.4 Scope of This Dissertation

This dissertation explores:

1. The crisis of verification in the age of synthetic abundance
2. The limitations of existing prediction markets
3. The missing "NO" button—permissionless shorting at launch
4. The architecture that enables it: intent-based, solver-centric OTC
5. The game theory of listings and control of the token lifecycle
6. The "Strait Gate" and the Thumbs Down for the tokenized economy
