## 4.3 Why Leverage Is Necessary

Truth is often held by the **minority**. In a democratic vote (1 person = 1 vote), the mob (10,000 people) beats the expert (1 scientist). In a leveraged market (1 dollar = 1 vote), the expert can use leverage to amplify conviction.

- 10,000 people put $10 into a Lie (Total $100k)
- 1 person knows the Truth but only has $10k
- **Without leverage**: The Lie wins (price goes up)
- **With 20× leverage**: The Truth Knower punches with $200k of force. The Lie is crushed.

Leverage allows those with high-certainty knowledge to overpower those with low-certainty hype.
