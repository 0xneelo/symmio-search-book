## 1.1 Two Threads Through Human History

Since the beginning of time, two threads have drawn themselves through humankind:

**The thread of Information**: Information underlies reality. It is more important than ever in human history. At the verge of artificial general intelligence, people are realizing what Google understood almost twenty years ago: **own the data, and you own the world.**

**The thread of Trade**: The creation and trading of assets of any kind. Trade has been the engine of human coordination, value exchange, and economic evolution.

These threads are not parallel—they are converging.
