## 5.2 Replication Conditions for Other Communities

For another token community to replicate this model, the following conditions are likely required:

- active trader base willing to express views via perps,
- credible market narrative and recurring participation,
- sufficient liquidity/risk controls for liquidation integrity,
- transparent LP reporting that builds depositor trust.
