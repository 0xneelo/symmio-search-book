## 6.7 Summary

Strategic implications span protocol designers, launchpads, order book protocols, CEXs, traders, and the broader ecosystem. The dominant theme is **partnership over competition**. A protocol that fills the lifecycle gap would likely be complementary to existing players rather than directly adversarial to them. Starting with low-cap perps and integrating into the existing lifecycle would be the most plausible path to durable value capture without zero-sum conflict.
