## 7.5 The Strait Gate (Matthew 7:13–14)

> "Enter ye in at the strait gate: for wide is the gate, and broad is the way, that leadeth to destruction, and many there be which go in thereat. Because strait is the gate, and narrow is the way, which leadeth unto life, and few there be that find it."

**The Wide Gate**: The status quo. Easy to launch tokens, fake volume, bribe committees. Many go in. Destination: destruction.

**The Strait Gate**: Vibe. To pass, an asset cannot just pay or know someone. It must survive the Solvers, the Short Sellers, the volatility. Few find it. Destination: life. The assets that survive are **alive**—proven demand, liquidity, resilience.

**The Power Flip**: You do not beg assets to list. Assets **aspire** to survive your protocol. "Verified on Vibe" becomes the ultimate badge. You hold the keys to the gate; you control the definition of what is Real.
