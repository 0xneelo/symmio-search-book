## 4.1 The Long-Only Flaw

The current meta (PumpFun) is flawed because it is **long-only**. In a long-only market, price is not a measure of truth—it is only a measure of hype. If you can only "Buy" or "Not Buy," the price can only go up or stay flat.

To create a true "Truth Market," you must be able to **profit from the destruction of a lie**. Permissionless futures and shorting at launch are the immune system that infinite information needs.
