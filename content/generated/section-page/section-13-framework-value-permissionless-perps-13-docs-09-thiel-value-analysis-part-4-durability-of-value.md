## Part 4: Durability of Value

### Thiel's Emphasis

> "We overvalue growth rates and undervalue durability."

### Vibe's Durability Factors

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| Structural advantage | ~100x capital efficiency; hard to replicate |
| Risk alignment | Sustainable LP economics |
| Defense hierarchy | Stress resilience |
| Lifecycle progression | Bootstrap → mature; reduces LP reliance over time |
| Partnership strategy | Launchpad, order book integration |
| 160+ partners | Early commitment; switching costs as they integrate |

### Threats to Durability

| Threat | Mitigation |
|--------|------------|
| Better architecture emerges | Vibe can adopt; first-mover data advantage |
| LP yield insufficient | 70% share; risk alignment; early traction suggests adequacy |
| Volume below expectations | Focus on high-interest tokens; expand partnerships |
| Competition | 18+ month build; structural moats |
