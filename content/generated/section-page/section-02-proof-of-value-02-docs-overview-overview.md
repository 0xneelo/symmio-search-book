## Overview

This dissertation presents the **Proof of Value** thesis: that market cap becomes the verification layer for truth in an era of infinite synthetic information. When the cost of creating information drops to zero, the only way to trust it is if it is tokenized and has a market cap attached. The thesis draws from the dialogue in `04_transcripts/04_context.md`.
