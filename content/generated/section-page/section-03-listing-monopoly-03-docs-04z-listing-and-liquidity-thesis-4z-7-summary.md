## 4Z.7 Summary

- **Permissionless listing** without **liquidity generation** is not the full thesis; many systems can create **markets** that nobody can trade.
- **Zero-cost liquidity generation** (in the Uniswap sense) means **mechanized quoting** from deposited capital—not a separate MM engagement per token.
- The hard problem is a **generalized**, **robust** liquidity layer for **many** perpetuals, including under **adversarial** conditions.
- **Durable power** aligns with **bootstrap + maturation + graduation**, not with symbolic market creation alone.
