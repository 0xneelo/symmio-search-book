# Ode To OrderBooks Source Navigation Map

Neelo's Ode to OrderBooks abstract, introduction thesis, overview, table of contents, core thesis, and source-marker fragments are source-navigation material for the order-book complementarity argument. They should route readers into the authored market-structure pages, not become seven separate public pages.

The core thesis is deliberately pro-order-book: order books are excellent for mature assets with continuous synchronous demand. Route readers who need that framing to `authored-order-books-as-mature-end-state`, and route readers asking why early long-tail markets need different architecture to `authored-order-book-bootstrap-limit`.

The abstract and dissertation overview explain Vibe's role as the missing upstream layer. Vibe should be described as discovering demand, hardening token lifecycles, and preparing markets for later order-book efficiency. Route demand-discovery questions to `authored-vibe-as-discovery-layer`, RFQ/intents questions to `authored-rfq-before-order-book`, and graduation questions to `authored-order-books-as-graduation-layer`.

The table of contents is a routing map for the public compendium. The sequence runs from order-book limits to Vibe's bootstrap layer, the market assembly line, lifecycle hardening, order-book complementarity, CLOB upgrade strategy, and the house-of-all-finance thesis. Route assembly-line questions to `authored-market-assembly-line`, hardening questions to `authored-lifecycle-hardening-before-listing`, Hyperliquid-gap questions to `authored-hyperliquid-gap-lower-layer`, and cooperation questions to `authored-improve-before-replacing`.

The source-marker fragments say the argument is based primarily on a transcript plus adjacent lifecycle-control, bootstrap-trilemma, and graduation strategy work. Treat that as provenance, not as a current implementation guarantee. It is especially important not to promote SSHE references, automatic routing, or direct Hyperliquid handoff language without current source and operator review.

## Publication Boundary

Direct Hyperliquid integrations, SSHE mechanics, automatic graduation rules, current order-book listing policies, market-maturity thresholds, venue economics, launchpad partnerships, and "house of all finance" product commitments require fresh primary-source, product, market, risk, legal, security, and operator review before publication as current facts.
