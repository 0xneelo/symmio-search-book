# Information Systems Need Market Validation

Neelo's one-primitive corpus starts from information, not trading. Speech, writing, print, computers, the internet, and AI each made information easier to preserve, distribute, process, or generate.

That history creates the current bottleneck. When generation is cheap, access is no longer the scarce resource. Validation is. The market needs mechanisms that make claims carry cost, not only attention.

This is why the Vibe x Symmio thesis should be framed as infrastructure for information validation. A spot market can express belief by buying. A derivatives market can also express disbelief, hedging demand, and stress. That makes the market a stronger verification surface for tokens, claims, narratives, and economic activity.

## Reader Implication

Do not reduce Vibe to "more leverage." The deeper thesis is that derivatives help information become market-tested. When creation becomes abundant, validation becomes the primitive.
