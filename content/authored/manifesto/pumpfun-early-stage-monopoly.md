# PumpFun As The Early-Stage Monopoly

Neelo uses PumpFun to show the opposite pole from Binance: early-stage lifecycle control.

PumpFun-style infrastructure dominates because it controls creation and graduation. It lowers the barrier to launching a token, gives projects a simple route into a bonding-curve market, and defines the rule by which the token graduates toward DEX trading.

That graduation rule is the key gate. Creation may feel permissionless, but the protocol still controls when and how a token moves from initial issuance into the next market stage. That gives the early-stage platform user capture, community alignment, and network effects.

The source also frames PumpFun's expansion into swap and DEX-like functionality as natural vertical integration. If a platform controls creation, it has an incentive to capture more of the trading flow after graduation.

## Binance Versus PumpFun

Binance and PumpFun occupy opposite poles of the barbell. Binance is late-stage and selective because mature venues cannot list everything. PumpFun is early-stage and permissive because its design can facilitate many fair launches.

The gap between them is the strategic opening. Early creation can be open, and late-stage liquidity can be deep, while the middle remains thin.
