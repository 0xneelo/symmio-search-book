# Strategic Implications For Launchpads And DEXs

For launchpads and DEXs, a gap-filling perp layer extends the value of graduation.

Launchpads can create and graduate tokens. DEXs can provide spot access and early liquidity. Neither has to become a full derivatives protocol to benefit from earlier perp access. Neelo's strategic point is that partnering with a permissionless perp layer can let upstream venues offer a longer lifecycle path without building all the infrastructure themselves.

## User Retention

Users who want leverage, short exposure, or hedging will go where those tools exist. If the first serious perp market appears only on a late-stage venue, users and attention can migrate there. If perp access appears soon after DEX graduation, the decentralized ecosystem keeps the market journey alive for longer.

That retention argument is not only about traders. Projects and communities can point to a clearer path: creation, spot, early derivatives, maturation, and then possible graduation to deeper venues.

## Differentiation

The source names the differentiator plainly: a launchpad can tell projects that graduation leads to derivative access. That is stronger than saying graduation merely creates a spot pair.

The strategic implication is that launchpads and DEXs do not need to treat a permissionless perp protocol as an external threat. It can become a value-added service that makes their own lifecycle gate more valuable.
