# Universal Perp Wedge

The Information/Trade source does not jump directly from verification crisis to every possible niche information market. It names a practical wedge first: the universal perp.

That wedge matters because perps are already legible to crypto traders, market makers, and listing venues. If Vibe can make early derivatives markets possible for assets that do not yet have mature order books, it can collect the first serious evidence of demand, risk, volatility, solver appetite, and short-side pressure.

The source frames the long tail aggressively: any DEX token, any market cap, any liquidity. The publication-safe version should keep the ambition while caveating the implementation. The compendium can say the wedge is broader long-tail perp access. It should not promise literal support for every token or every liquidity condition until eligibility, oracle, solver, margin, and risk filters are confirmed.

The strategic order is important. Universal perps are the wedge because they are a familiar derivatives primitive. More specialized information or reputation markets can come later if the system proves that solvers, vaults, and risk controls can price the long tail.

## Reader Implication

The universal-perp wedge explains why Vibe can begin as market infrastructure before becoming a broader information-verification layer. Perps provide the first tractable surface for long-tail market formation.

## Publication Boundary

The source supports the long-tail perp access thesis, not a live promise of support for every DEX token, market cap, liquidity condition, oracle setup, margin mode, leverage level, or solver/risk-control configuration.
