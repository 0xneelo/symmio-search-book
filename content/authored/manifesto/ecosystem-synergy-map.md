# Ecosystem Synergy Map

Neelo's ecosystem-synergy section frames Vibe as connective infrastructure. The thesis is not that Vibe must replace every other market surface. It is that early perp markets can make adjacent systems more useful by giving them a market-tested derivative layer.

Order-book protocols are the clearest example. A downstream CLOB venue is strongest when a market has real flow, maker interest, and enough history to support tight execution. Vibe can sit upstream as the bootstrap layer that discovers which assets deserve that mature structure.

Lending protocols have a different synergy. If long-tail assets can be borrowed, lent, and hedged, more capital strategies become possible. The source's example is delta-neutral or hedged use of assets that previously lacked practical derivative surfaces.

Launchpads benefit because token creation does not need to end at spot-only speculation. A token can move from launch to spot to early perp testing, creating more evidence for whether the market should survive.

Data providers benefit because the market emits information: real demand indicators, risk metrics, behavior analytics, and price-discovery quality scores.

## Publication Boundary

This is an ecosystem map, not a partner announcement. Do not claim live integrations with specific order-book venues, lending protocols, launchpads, or data providers unless separately confirmed by public source or operator approval.
