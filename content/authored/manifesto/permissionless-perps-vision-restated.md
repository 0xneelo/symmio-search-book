# The Permissionless Perps Vision Restated

Neelo's conclusion restates the vision in its most direct form: permissionless perpetual markets are not only a product feature. They are a missing layer of market infrastructure.

The source contrasts today's lifecycle with the desired one. Today, tokens can be launched easily, but only a small subset earn deep derivatives markets. Listing decisions are still often relationship-driven, opaque, or delayed until demand is already obvious. That leaves a gap between token creation, spot access, and credible derivatives.

The Vibe thesis is that this gap should become continuous. A token should be able to express derivative demand earlier, produce market data, mature under risk controls, and graduate when objective evidence supports it.

This page is the concise answer to why the compendium exists. The goal is not to make every token look safe or valuable. The goal is to make market creation legible enough that good markets can prove themselves and weak markets can be tested rather than narrated into legitimacy.

## Publication Boundary

Do not publish exact live market counts, universal availability, automatic graduation, or relationship-free listing outcomes as current facts without fresh product evidence. The source supports the direction: complete the token lifecycle with permissionless, evidence-producing derivatives.
