# The Self-Correcting Token Market Stack

The final stack in Neelo's corpus is simple: programmable issuance, permissionless spot trading, and permissionless derivatives.

The third layer is what makes the stack self-correcting. Issuance lets assets appear. Spot trading lets buyers form a market. Derivatives let the market express both belief and skepticism, hedge risk, and mature through pressure rather than only narrative momentum.

This is where Vibe and Symmio are paired in the source. Symmio supplies derivatives infrastructure and settlement machinery. Vibe supplies the product and permissionless market layer that can bring perps to the long tail of tokens.

## What This Does Not Mean

It does not mean every token deserves reckless leverage or permanent support. It means every meaningful token should have a path to being tested. Some markets will prove themselves and graduate. Others will fail earlier because the negative side can finally act.

## Publication Note

The source supports the strategic division between Vibe and Symmio. Final production docs still need platform, product, and operator review before making promises about exact market creation workflow, graduation policy, solver responsibilities, or vault exposure.
