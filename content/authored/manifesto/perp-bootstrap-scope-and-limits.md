# The Perp Bootstrap Paper's Scope And Limits

The Perp Classes / Z-Score paper is deliberately scoped. It focuses on perpetual futures markets in crypto, not every derivative product and not every financial market.

That matters for publication discipline. The paper can support claims about perp market bootstrap, matching architecture, collateralization, insurance topology, and market lifecycle. It does not by itself settle options design, prediction-market design, regulatory treatment, or traditional-finance applicability.

The source also assumes a technically literate reader: basic derivatives, DeFi primitives, smart contracts, and oracle concepts. The compendium should make those ideas navigable, but the paper's own claims sit inside that domain.

The page is therefore a guardrail for the rest of the manifesto. It lets the docs say, with conviction, what the source argues, while refusing to smuggle in broader promises the source did not make.

## Publication Boundary

Use this page whenever a reader asks how far the bootstrap thesis reaches. Do not infer legal conclusions, options-market applicability, prediction-market applicability, traditional-finance portability, or live implementation guarantees from the introduction alone.
