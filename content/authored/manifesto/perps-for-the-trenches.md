# Perps For The Trenches

"Perps for the trenches" means derivative access reaches tokens while perceived interest is still low.

In the Game Theory of Listings source, the trenches are not only small-cap speculation. They are the part of the market where actual interest has not yet been revealed, future winners are hard to distinguish from noise, and no high-cost curated venue can comfortably justify early validation.

Vibe is strategically important because it pushes derivatives earlier in that lifecycle. A token does not need elite approval before the market can test, hedge, short, and price it. That does not make every asset good. It makes the market better at discovering which assets are good, dangerous, fragile, ignored, or overhyped.

The conclusion of the source is that listing design is a theory of market evolution. A system can wait for obvious demand and reduce embarrassment, or it can let markets discover demand before it is obvious. Vibe's place is the second path: an anti-bottleneck layer between permissionless creation and mature venue validation.

## Publication Boundary

This page should not be read as a live guarantee that every token can immediately receive a production Vibe market. The source supports the strategic thesis. Final docs still need product-owner confirmation for exact listing workflow, eligibility rules, solver support, vault exposure, and graduation policy.
