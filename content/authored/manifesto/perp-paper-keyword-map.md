# The Perp Bootstrap Paper Keyword Map

The abstract keywords are a useful map of the paper's intellectual territory. They place the argument at the intersection of perpetual futures, market microstructure, automated market makers, order books, decentralized finance, market bootstrap, liquidity provision, and derivatives trading.

That mix matters. The source is not only about a perp venue, not only about a solver, and not only about DeFi liquidity. It asks how a derivative market comes into existence, how the first counterparty problem is handled, and how the design changes as liquidity matures.

The keyword set also keeps the compendium honest about complementarity. Automated market makers and order books both appear because the paper is not written as a simple "AMM versus CLOB" argument. It is about which mechanism fits which market state and how a market can move between states.

## Reader Implication

When a reader asks "what field is this paper in?", route here before sending them into the full framework. The short answer is: market bootstrap for perpetual futures, using market microstructure as the organizing language.
