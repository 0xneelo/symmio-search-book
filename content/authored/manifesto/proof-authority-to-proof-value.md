# From Proof Of Authority To Proof Of Value

Neelo's Proof of Value source begins with a verification break. In the pre-AI internet, authority signals still had some friction: experts, brands, outlets, production cost, and reputation could act as rough filters. Once synthetic content becomes cheap, those filters weaken. A polished article, video, pitch deck, or fake expert can be produced without much commitment.

Proof of Value is the proposed replacement filter. Instead of asking only who said something, the market asks what has been staked behind it and whether it can survive adversarial trading.

That shift is why this compendium connects information and derivatives. A tokenized claim with liquid markets is harder to treat as free speech alone. Supporters can buy. Skeptics can short. Solvers can refuse to quote. LPs can demand risk premium. The market becomes a costly test of belief rather than a passive channel for authority.

## Reader Implication

When a reader asks why Vibe's docs talk about truth, markets, and information, the answer should not be mystical. The thesis is that authority is becoming easier to fake, so the system needs costly signals. Vibe and Symmio matter because they can make those signals two-sided, collateralized, and inspectable.
