# Ungated Derivatives Access

The last-primitive claim is about access to market structure.

Neelo's source defines the missing primitive as permissionless derivatives for any token. That means a token can receive a perpetual market without first passing through centralized listing committees, gatekept market makers, or late-stage order-book approval.

The point is not that every token should receive unlimited leverage. The point is that market testing should not depend only on elite venue permission.

## What The Primitive Changes

Ungated derivatives access gives long-tail tokens a path to two-sided price discovery earlier in their lifecycle. A market can express enthusiasm, skepticism, hedge demand, and stress before a mature venue has enough evidence to list it.

That is where Vibe and Symmio are paired in the source: Symmio supplies derivatives infrastructure and settlement machinery, while Vibe supplies the product and permissionless market layer for assets that the rest of the industry does not yet serve.

## Publication Boundary

This page documents the strategic primitive. It does not finalize current Vibe listing eligibility, solver support, vault exposure, market approval process, leverage limits, or launch workflow.
