# Trust Flows Upstream

If Vibe repeatedly sends better markets into mature execution venues, trust can begin to move toward the layer where market discovery starts.

That is the strategic meaning of the CLOB-upgrade thesis. At first, traders and communities may trust Vibe because it feeds order books with better candidates. Over time, they may trust Vibe because it is where the earliest perp expression, short interest, solver pricing, and market-quality data first become legible.

This is not a branding claim. It is a market-structure claim. A protocol that owns upstream trust can influence which assets become serious, which signals matter, and how early markets are evaluated before they reach final execution layers.

## Why It Matters

Downstream trust is valuable, but it is reactive. The market already exists, and the venue competes on execution. Upstream trust is more powerful because it shapes the path by which a market becomes mature enough to deserve execution at scale.

For Vibe, the compendium should make this path concrete: earn trust by improving downstream outcomes, then become the place where new derivatives markets first become understandable.
