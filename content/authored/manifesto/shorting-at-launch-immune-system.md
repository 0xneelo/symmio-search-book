# Shorting At Launch As Market Immune System

The source uses immune-system language for permissionless shorting at launch. The reason is timing. If skepticism arrives only after a token, story, or claim has already matured, the market may have rewarded falsehood for too long.

Launch-time shorting makes the negative side part of the initial market test. A new asset or narrative is not only asking whether buyers will appear. It is also asking whether skeptics can find enough evidence, conviction, and execution capacity to push back.

That matters in a world of cheap synthetic information. False narratives can move quickly. If the market has no early short side, the correction mechanism is delayed. If the short side exists from the beginning, falsehood has to survive pressure earlier.

This is a thesis page, not a guarantee that every newly issued asset will receive live shorting, identical leverage, or instant Vibe market support. Eligibility, oracle readiness, solver appetite, inventory, margin, and risk limits remain product and implementation decisions.

## Reader Implication

Shorting at launch is the proposed negative feedback loop for new tokenized claims. It lets a market test support and rejection at the same time instead of making skepticism wait for maturity.
