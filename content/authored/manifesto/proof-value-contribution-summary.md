# Proof Of Value Contribution Summary

Neelo's Proof of Value conclusion is useful because it compresses the whole framework into a contribution map.

The source says the paper contributes five linked claims. First, Vibe should be evaluated through four constituencies: LPs, traders, projects, and the wider ecosystem. Second, LP value depends on token inventory, revenue participation, capital efficiency, and risk alignment. Third, traders and projects receive different forms of value: traders get long-tail perp access and clearer payout paths, while projects get a way to turn token inventory into market infrastructure without leading with stablecoin spend.

The fourth contribution is economic clarity. The source does not treat risk as a vague socialized pool. It says directional PnL belongs to traders, residual exposure is warehoused and hedged by the solver side, and tail outcomes follow a disclosed defense or risk waterfall. The fifth contribution is comparative advantage: the hybrid path is framed against external USDC vaults and token-margined inverse designs.

## Why This Page Exists
