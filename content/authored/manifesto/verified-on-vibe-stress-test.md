# Verified On Vibe As A Stress-Test Badge

The Proof of Value source imagines Vibe as more than a venue. It imagines Vibe as a filter that later venues and communities can trust.

The phrase "Verified on Vibe" should not mean that Vibe certifies objective truth. It should mean that a market survived a stress test: derivative demand, short pressure, volatility, solver pricing, liquidation behavior, repeated flow, and the discipline of capital at risk.

That is a stronger badge than a paid listing or a narrative campaign. A market that only persuades a committee has shown political or marketing ability. A market that survives Vibe has produced market evidence.

## Publication Boundary

The source includes example thresholds and downstream graduation paths. Those should not become public product rules until confirmed. This page documents the thesis, not a live badge program, eligibility policy, or guarantee of downstream listings.

## Reader Implication

For projects, the right question becomes: can the market earn evidence? For venues, the useful question becomes: what did this asset prove under two-sided derivatives pressure? For Vibe, the strategic opportunity is to become the stress-test layer that turns early markets into legible candidates for deeper liquidity.
