# Vibe As Listing Source Of Truth

The Information/Trade source ties verification back to listing power. Listing committees and auction systems often decide from perceived value: narrative, relationships, marketing, deposits, and expected flow. That creates an incentive to look valuable before the market has proven that it is valuable.

Vibe's role in the source is to create a different kind of evidence. If a token receives early perp access, then downstream venues can observe actual derivative demand, short interest, solver appetite, volatility, quote quality, liquidation behavior, and persistence. That record is harder to fake than a campaign built only for a committee.

The source gives example thresholds and a mechanical graduation path from launchpad to DEX to Vibe to order book. Those examples should not be published as live rules without product confirmation. The durable idea is the listing-source-of-truth function: Vibe can be the stress-test layer that turns "we have a strong narrative" into "we survived a market with leverage, shorts, solvers, and volatility."

This also sharpens the relationship with mature venues. Vibe does not need to replace every later venue to own value. If downstream listings increasingly use Vibe data as evidence, then Vibe owns a critical part of the discovery and verification path.

## Reader Implication

Market creators should treat early perp demand as proof they can earn, not a badge they receive from relationships alone. Downstream venues should see Vibe data as a filter that can reduce listing guesswork.
