# Tokens Are Information Objects

The one-primitive corpus treats tokens as more than ownership wrappers. A token can contain information about a project, community, claim, event, outcome, or productive capacity.

That is why token issuance matters. Crypto did not only make financial assets easier to create; it made information easier to financialize. Once something can become a token, it can attract capital, attention, speculation, and coordination.

But an information object needs a validation path. Spot trading tells the market who wants exposure. Derivatives tell the market how much conviction survives pressure, hedge demand, short interest, and maturity. A token without that second layer remains partially legible.

## Reader Implication

Readers should understand why Vibe's market surface matters even for nontraditional assets. If tokens are information objects, then permissionless derivatives are one way to test the information with capital.
