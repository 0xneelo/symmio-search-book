# Autonomous Market Creation

Autonomous market creation is the positive version of the market-creation gap. Instead of asking which markets deserve to exist, the system lets markets begin in a constrained form and then become more efficient if real demand appears.

The source thesis has six moving parts: any token can begin with a market, the first state can be inefficient but functional, traders make the market deeper over time, objective maturity metrics track progress, graduation can happen by transparent criteria, and no human listing desk has to decide in advance which assets deserve a derivatives venue.

That matters because it changes what "permissionless" means. It is not just permissionless contract deployment. It is permissionless market testing with an economic path from first trade to mature venue.

## Reader Implication

This page should orient founders, treasuries, and traders around Vibe as a market-lifecycle machine. A market starts because someone wants exposure; it matures because enough flow, balance, and liquidity show up.
