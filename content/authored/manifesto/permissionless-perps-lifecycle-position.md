# Where Permissionless Perps Sit In The Lifecycle

Neelo positions permissionless perps in the missing middle of the token lifecycle.

The protocol class is not aimed at brand-new tokens before any spot trading exists. It is also not aimed at the deepest mature markets that already qualify for major order-book or CEX derivatives venues. It sits between those poles: tokens already trading on DEXs, but not yet served by serious perp infrastructure.

Section 4Z is important here. The source says "permissionless perpetuals" cannot mean only that anyone can open a market. The market also needs tradeable liquidity. Without a bootstrap path, a permissionless perp is only an empty listing object.

## The Lifecycle Slot

The lifecycle slot is:

launch or fair launch -> DEX spot -> permissionless perp bootstrap -> mature order-book or CEX derivative venue.

That slot gives Vibe's thesis a disciplined shape. It is not trying to replace every downstream venue. It is trying to create the missing layer that lets demand become measurable before a downstream venue can justify deeper execution.

## Publication Boundary

This page supports a strategic lifecycle position. It does not confirm current Vibe eligibility, universal market creation, automatic graduation, downstream venue handoff, or exact bootstrap liquidity obligations.
