# The Bootstrap Limit Of Order Books

Order books are selective because they have to be. A full order-book market requires infrastructure, market making, operational support, liquidity attention, and enough continuous interest to justify the surface.

That creates the long-tail gap. Crypto can create assets permissionlessly, but mature derivative listings remain scarce. A venue must decide which markets are worth supporting before the market has fully expressed whether traders actually want the perp.

The result is a prediction problem. Venues use proxies: spot volume, community pressure, relationships, auction outcomes, narrative strength, prior listings, or other signals of perceived demand. Projects then optimize for those proxies because the proxy controls market access.

The source's claim is structural, not moral. Even a well-intentioned order-book venue becomes a gatekeeper when it cannot cheaply test every market.

## Reader Implication

The docs should explain that Vibe is solving the information gap before mature listing. It lets demand become observable before the order-book layer has to commit scarce listing and liquidity resources.
