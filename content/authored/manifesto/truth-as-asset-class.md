# Truth As An Asset Class

The Proof of Value source uses intentionally provocative language: truth stops being only a statement and starts behaving like a market object.

The safe reading is not that markets create objective truth. The safe reading is that markets can make belief costly, continuous, and inspectable. A claim that attracts capital, survives short pressure, keeps liquidity, and matures through volatility has passed a different test from a claim that only circulates as content.

That is why the compendium should treat "truth as an asset class" as a thesis about verification infrastructure. The relevant asset is not certainty. It is capitalized belief under pressure.

## What Vibe Adds

Spot markets let believers buy. They do not fully test whether the claim can survive rejection. Vibe's role is to add the derivative layer: leverage, shorting, solver refusal, risk pricing, and market history around claims or assets that would otherwise remain long-only narratives.

This is also why the thesis needs guardrails. A high market cap can be manipulated. Wealth can overpower quieter evidence. Markets are costly signals, not moral arbiters. The strongest public version is: truth-like claims become more credible when they survive open, two-sided, capital-at-risk markets.
