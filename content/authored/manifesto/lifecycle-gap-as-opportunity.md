# The Lifecycle Gap As Opportunity

Neelo's opportunity claim follows directly from the gap diagnosis.

If tokens can graduate into DEX spot long before they receive perp access, then the missing interval is not only a market defect. It is a strategic opening for a protocol that can make derivatives tradeable before conventional venues are ready.

The source names four parts of that opening: an underserved asset band, clear demand for exposure and protection, strategic value in connecting early and late stages, and first-mover advantage because incumbents do not naturally serve the segment.

## What The Opportunity Is

The opportunity is not "list every token forever."

It is to provide a bridge where spot demand already exists but mature derivatives are absent. Traders want leverage or short expression. Holders want hedges. Projects want market validation. Later venues want evidence. A protocol that can serve that interval can become the layer where derivative demand is first observed.

## Publication Boundary

The source uses approximate market-cap bands and first-mover language. Treat those as strategic framework claims, not current Vibe eligibility, market-size promises, live demand proof, or investment claims. Exact addressable market and product thresholds need operator/product/risk/legal review.
