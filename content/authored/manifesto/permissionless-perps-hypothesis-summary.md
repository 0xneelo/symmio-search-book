# Permissionless Perps Hypothesis Summary

Section 5 treats permissionless perps as the missing lifecycle layer between DEX graduation and major venue eligibility.

The summary is deliberately hypothetical. If a protocol can systematically create perp markets for tokens in that interval, it can fill a structural gap, make the lifecycle continuous, and occupy a strategically powerful bridge position.

The section also clarifies what the hypothesis is not. It is not a claim that every token deserves leverage, that every early market should be permanent, or that the protocol replaces downstream venues. It is a claim that the empty middle is large enough and important enough to deserve its own market-formation layer.

## How This Sets Up The Next Section

The next move is strategic implication. If the gap exists and a protocol can fill it, then each surrounding venue changes role. Launchpads become upstream supply. DEXs become spot evidence. Order books become graduation surfaces. The gap-filling layer becomes the place where long-tail derivatives demand first becomes measurable.
