# Proof Of Value Thesis Boundary

The Proof of Value thesis says Vibe's hybrid architecture can create sustainable economic value for LPs, traders, and projects, but the compendium must separate thesis from production proof.

Neelo's source states the thesis in strong terms: token-vault inventory, solver-funded stablecoin operations, active risk management, and lifecycle progression together form a structurally superior path for low-cap perpetuals. It also includes model claims about capital efficiency, risk alignment, and early partner commitment.

The publication boundary is just as important. The same introduction says the paper focuses on economic value creation and structural sustainability. It does not settle smart-contract security, jurisdiction-specific compliance, or detailed mathematical derivations.

## How The Docs Should Use The Thesis

Use the thesis as the argument spine, not as a blanket proof. It should route readers into the pages that can substantiate each piece:

- LP value and token inventory;
- trader execution and payout confidence;
- project utility and participation;
- solver-funded USDC operations;
- risk alignment and defense hierarchy;
- validation, sustainability, and market maturity.

If one of those pieces lacks current source evidence, the docs should say so and keep the claim review-bound.

## Publication Boundary

Do not publish source-time partner counts, committed value, capital-efficiency ratios, live risk alignment, security assurances, regulatory conclusions, or mathematical finality as settled production facts without fresh primary-source/operator/product/risk/legal/accounting/security review.
