# The Initial Market For Permissionless Perps

Neelo's Thiel-style beachhead is low-cap perps for tokens that have graduated into spot trading but remain below major perp-listing thresholds.

That beachhead is narrow by design. It is not the whole global derivatives market, and it is not every freshly created token. It is the lifecycle segment where spot demand exists, derivative demand is plausible, and incumbent venues do not systematically serve the market.

## Why This Beachhead Is Coherent

The source names four reasons the beachhead works as a monopoly starting point.

It is clearly bounded by lifecycle position. It is underserved by existing venues. It is winnable because a focused protocol could plausibly become the default venue for the niche. It is expandable because adjacent token classes, chains, and later-stage markets can be reached after the first segment is understood.

That is the strategic discipline behind the permissionless-perps hypothesis. The strongest starting point is not maximum surface area. It is a small market with a real pain, a missing venue category, and a path to expansion.

## Publication Boundary

Treat this as source-backed strategy, not final Vibe eligibility. Exact market-cap bands, token classes, supported chains, launch criteria, leverage, liquidity requirements, and public listing policy need fresh operator/product/risk/legal/implementation review.
