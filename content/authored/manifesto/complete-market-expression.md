# Complete Market Expression

Complete market expression means a token can be more than bought or ignored.

Neelo's source says spot markets are powerful but incomplete. A spot market lets participants buy an asset, sell inventory, or abstain. It does not give every token a native path to be shorted, hedged, stress-tested, basis-traded, or matured under market pressure.

That missing side is why narratives can outrun fundamentals. The positive side of the market acts earlier than the negative and hedging side.

## Why Derivatives Matter

Derivatives add the missing market language. They let participants express disbelief without needing to own the token first. They let holders hedge. They let markets measure conviction under leverage, funding, liquidation, and risk limits.

The source's point is not that derivatives guarantee truth. It is narrower and stronger: a token without derivatives has incomplete price discovery because a large part of market opinion has no direct expression.

## Reader Implication

When a reader asks why perps matter beyond leverage, route them here. Derivatives complete market expression by adding skepticism, hedging, and stress to the token's public market life.
