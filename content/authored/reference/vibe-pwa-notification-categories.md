# Vibe PWA Notification Categories

The Vibe PWA notification list is a product-surface map, not a replacement for account monitoring.

The official mobile guide lists push notifications for opened trades, closed trades, referral updates, liquidations, new features, rewards, and newly added trading pairs. It also says users can configure which notifications they receive in app settings.

## What Notifications Do And Do Not Prove

Notifications tell the user which event categories the app is designed to surface. They do not prove that a trader can ignore account health, margin, liquidation distance, funding, or open-position review.

For liquidation-sensitive questions, the answer should route from this page to account health and portfolio pages. For referral or reward notifications, final public economics still need the relevant referral and points caveats.
