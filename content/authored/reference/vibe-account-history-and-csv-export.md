# Vibe Account History And CSV Export

Vibe's My Account page is the reference surface for account history.

The official portfolio guide says My Account includes transaction history, deposit and withdrawal history, detailed PnL stats, daily and cumulative PnL charts, funding charts, trading-fee charts, rakeback tracking, account health, referral-code activity, points, and CSV export.

That makes it the right page to answer "where did this number come from?" questions. Deposits and withdrawals explain balance movement. Fees and funding explain trading-cost movement. PnL charts explain performance over time. Account health explains risk capacity.

## What CSV Export Means

CSV export gives the user a recordkeeping output. The docs should not imply that exporting a CSV solves tax treatment, accounting classification, or jurisdiction-specific reporting. The source supports data export, not legal or tax interpretation.

The practical answer is:

- use My Account for transaction history and account-state review;
- use the fee and funding charts to explain trading-cost changes;
- export CSV when the user needs raw records for their own workflow;
- route tax/accounting interpretation outside the current source set.
