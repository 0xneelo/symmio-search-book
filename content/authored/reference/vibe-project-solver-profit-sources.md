# Vibe Project Solver Profit Sources

The official system visualization names multiple solver profit channels for project-side perp listings.

It lists spread, funding rates, liquidations, and losing trades as solver profit sources. The project-listing terms then broaden the economic vocabulary by distinguishing operational profits from opportunistic revenues and by keeping distribution mechanics agreement-specific.

## Why This Matters

Solver economics are not one fee line. A project-side listing can create market-making and risk-management economics through:

- spread or quote economics;
- funding-rate effects;
- liquidation-related flows;
- trader losses or trading-result effects;
- other agreement-defined operational or opportunistic revenue categories.

That list should not be presented as a guaranteed project payout. It should be presented as the official source's map of possible profit channels around a market-making service.

## Publication Boundary

The operator still needs to approve the final public revenue disclosure boundary.
