# Referral Category Partner Overlays

The LP and category layer allows strategic partner programs at the category level. A tagged group of markets can carry an elevated platform fee, with the uplift split between the platform and the partner.

This is distinct from user-level referral rewards. A category partner overlay is a commercial and market-supply program attached to a market group. It should be tracked separately so users do not mistake a partner category rule for the public default referral table.

Category overlays can be useful when a partner brings distribution, listings, or a coherent market vertical. They also create disclosure risk if the fee uplift or split is hidden inside general referral wording.

## Publication Boundary

The docs can define category overlays as separate from public user-level referral policy. They should not publish live partner identities, tagged categories, uplift rates, fee splits, campaign economics, or private terms until commercial, legal, and accounting review approve the public statement.
