# Vibe Project Listing Terms

The project listing terms page is not ordinary product copy. It is the public terms source for projects that are listing, or considering listing, a token as a perpetual on Vibe. The compendium should summarize its operating model without turning it into legal advice.

## Service Model

The terms frame the relationship as a market-making and liquidity service for a token issuer or project. The service can include creating a perpetual market on Vibe/Symmio, supporting liquidity, price stabilization, and orderly trading activity, with KPIs specified in the relevant statement of work.

The source also gives the client an audit path: the client can request access to trading records and PnL statements with prior written notice, using a mutually agreed third-party auditor under confidentiality obligations, with audit costs borne by the client.

## Custody And Risk

The terms say liquidity tokens and related digital assets are provided to the market maker for the services, held as client assets, and accounted separately from the market maker's own assets. They also include a custody-risk disclaimer: no custody method or smart-contract arrangement is entirely risk-free.

The page describes a discretionary security fund concept, but also states that it is not a legally segregated or guaranteed reserve. That distinction is important for publication: the docs can explain the control, but should not imply guaranteed insurance coverage.

## Profit, Conduct, And Exit Mechanics

The terms distinguish operational profits, such as spreads, fees, funding, borrowing costs, and liquidation fees, from opportunistic revenues related to maintenance margin, trading results, and rebalancing. Profit split and distribution mechanics are agreement-specific, and the source says no profit, return, volume, liquidity, price performance, or market outcome is guaranteed.

The terms also include conduct restrictions against market manipulation, client information obligations, termination rights, community notice and wind-down language around delisting, final reporting, and Swiss-law arbitration terms.

## Reader Implication

Projects should read this page before treating Vibe listing as a simple self-serve ticker request. Traders should read it as background for how project-side inventory and market-making services can support a Vibe perp, not as a guarantee of any specific market's terms, liquidity, or profit split.

## Publication Boundary

This page summarizes public terms and is not legal advice. Do not publish exact KPIs, custody procedures, security-fund coverage, profit splits, supply commitments, conduct remedies, jurisdictional conclusions, or project-specific commercial terms as universal policy without legal, accounting, product, and operator review.
