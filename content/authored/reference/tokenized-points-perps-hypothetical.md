# Tokenized Points Perps Are A Hypothetical Scenario

The tokenized-points-perps section is not a product announcement. It is a thought experiment about what could become possible if reward-linked assets become on-chain economic objects and third-party builders compose around them.

The hypothetical flow is: users earn and claim points, points can be held or converted into packs and artifacts, an independent contract could fractionalize pack exposure, the resulting token could receive spot liquidity, and a permissionless perp venue could list that token if it met market requirements.

That scenario is useful because it shows why Vibe's reward architecture cannot be documented casually. Once points become transferable or wrapped, they can become financial primitives. Financial primitives can become markets. Markets can then amplify both legitimate hedging and manipulation risk.

## Required Labeling

Any final docs that mention this scenario must preserve three labels:

- hypothetical;
- third-party or community-built unless explicitly endorsed;
- not the default intended user flow.
