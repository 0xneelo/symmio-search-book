# SYMM LP Headline Result Shape

The SYMM LP case-study headline is not a single yield number. It is a shape with several parts: token balance increased, realized LP profit was positive, unrealized LP profit was positive at the data cut, and passive SYMM holding underperformed during the same measured window.

The source reports `2,271,131` SYMM deposited across three transactions and a current token balance of `2,501,328.4` SYMM. That is a token-count gain of `230,197.4` SYMM, or `10.14%` versus deposited tokens. It also reports `Current Debt` as `$5,895.90` realized LP profit and `Current UPnL` as `$7,999.32` unrealized LP profit at mark.

## Why The Shape Matters

Those numbers should not be collapsed into one promise. Token-count gain, realized USDC-denominated profit, unrealized mark, and passive-token benchmark spread are different claims.

The headline supports a proof-of-mechanism statement: in this observed long-skew down market, LP-side economics were favorable. It does not prove a durable APR, a risk-free vault, or a universal result across all token communities.

## Reader Implication

When a reader asks what the case study showed, answer in layers. The LP did not merely "earn yield." The measured state combined more SYMM tokens, positive realized debt owed to the LP, positive marked UPnL for the LP side, and a favorable comparison against passive SYMM holding.

That is stronger than vague marketing language and safer than overclaiming. It lets the case demonstrate the mechanism while leaving regime, attribution, drawdown, and replication questions open for the next pages.

## Publication Boundary

Do not publish these figures as current live vault performance, future APY, or a generalized investor claim. Final public wording needs operator/accounting review for audited attribution, time window, dashboard source, drawdowns, and whether any current product surface exposes the same fields.
