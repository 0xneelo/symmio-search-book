# Vibe Portfolio And Account Data

The My Account page is the product's practical operating ledger. It is where a trader checks current exposure, recent performance, transaction history, account health, and reward context without leaving the trading product.

## Account Overview

The official portfolio guide says My Account includes open-position summaries, PnL overviews for 24-hour, 7-day, and 30-day windows, deposit and withdrawal history, balance and account-health data, collateral usage, referral-code activity and earnings, and a Vibe Points summary.

That makes the page a bridge between trading, funding, risk, referrals, and points. The compendium should not document those as separate silos: each one becomes useful when the user can see how it changed their account state.

## Performance Analytics

The same source describes detailed PnL stats, daily and cumulative PnL charts, funding charts, trading-fee charts, rakeback tracking, transaction history, and CSV export for tax or accounting workflows.

Those features should be documented as analytics and recordkeeping surfaces, not as a guarantee that every tax or accounting treatment is solved by the product. CSV export gives the user data; jurisdiction-specific interpretation remains outside this source set.

## Reader Implication

When a user asks where to understand their account, route them to My Account first. When they ask why a number moved, route to the specific source of movement: positions and PnL, funding, fees, deposits/withdrawals, referral activity, points, or account-health changes.
