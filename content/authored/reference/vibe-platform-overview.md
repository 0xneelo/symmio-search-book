# Vibe Platform Overview

The public platform page explains Vibe as an accessible on-chain derivatives UI built for different trader profiles without removing advanced features. It connects that product posture to cross-chain communication, embedded wallets, account abstraction, and SYMMIO.

## Product Claims

The page says Vibe offers hundreds of markets, high leverage, permissionless lowcap perp listings, email or wallet account creation, deposits from EVM chains and Solana, a pre-TGE points program, revenue-share positioning for `$VIBE` holders, and trading-fee rakeback.

Several of those claims are time-sensitive or owner-controlled. Exact market counts, leverage ceilings, revenue-share wording, and deposit coverage should be re-verified on publication date and aligned with the current product and legal stance before final release.

## Role In The Compendium

This page is the public-product bridge between the manifesto and the operating guides. The manifesto explains why Vibe exists; the platform page explains what the user sees: markets, account creation, deposits, points, rakeback, and accessible trading workflows.
