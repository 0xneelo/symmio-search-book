# Vibe Project Token Custody Boundary

Project-supplied tokens are part of the market-creation workflow, so custody language has to be exact.

The project-listing terms say liquidity tokens and related digital assets are provided to the market maker for the services, held as client assets, and accounted separately from the market maker's own assets. The same terms include a custody-risk disclaimer: no custody method or smart-contract arrangement is entirely risk-free.

## Security Fund Caveat

The terms also describe a discretionary security fund concept. The important caveat is that the source does not frame it as a legally segregated or guaranteed reserve.

That means the docs can explain that a security-fund concept exists in the terms, but they should not describe it as guaranteed insurance, legal segregation, or an unconditional project protection mechanism.

## Reader Guidance

For projects, this page answers "what happens to the tokens we provide?" at the source level: client-asset accounting, service use, custody risk, and a non-guaranteed security-fund concept. It does not replace the actual agreement or legal review.
